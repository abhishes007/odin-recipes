Oreo Mug Cake

What is Oreo Mug Cake?

This is a viral 3-ingredient Oreo dessert recipe.Takes less than 5 minutes to make, perfect if you've got cravings for chocolate and you want it now!

Ingredients

  • 4 cream-filled chocolate sandwich cookies (such as OreoÂ®), crushed into fine crumbs
  • 3 tablespoons whole milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder

Steps to Cook

  1. Grease a small mug with a bit of butter, oil, or non-stick cooking spray.
  2. Mix Oreo cookie crumbs, whole milk, and baking powder in a small bowl until well combined.
  3. Pour into the prepared mug and microwave for 1 1/2 minutes. Let it cool down!
